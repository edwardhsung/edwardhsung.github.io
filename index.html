About Me

In my professional life, I am a mobile and web developer with a penchant for providing a straightforward and engaging user experience. I work in multi-disciplinary teams to build a platform with hardware, firmware, and software components; bringing these from concept to commercialization.

I bring together business and technical aspects of a project and help teams to communicate, smoothing over rough spots. In the past few years I have jumped back and forth between mobile (react-native), web/server (javascript, graphql, C#), and firmware (C++) development. And I strive to keep our focus on user needs.

In work and my personal life, I thrive on helping people identify their goals and encourage them to achieve them. I have worked with others within my church to build a framework for training and equipping new leaders. In addition, I am working to make beneficial changes to our church culture that improves support for our leaders; thereby increasing their satisfaction and effectiveness, while reducing frustration and burn out.
